I am a 2nd year PhD student in the Camera Culture group at the MIT Media Lab, advised by Ramesh Raskar. I also work with Brian Cheung and Tomaso Poggio's group. I recieved my S.M'23 from MIT and BS'19 ECE from Univeristy of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

My research focuses on AI-based computational discovery. I am currently focused on discovery in the context of vision: How can we use AI to study of biological visual intelligence (eg. What if Machine for Vision, Generative Design of Visual Intelligence)? How can we use biological principles to discover new forms of artificial visual intelligence (eg. Designing Cameras using RL)? Additionally, I am also interested in building AI tools that accelerate scientific discovery.

I am also a volunteer for the Media Lab's SOS Program and the EECS Graduate Application Assistance Program (GAAP). If you're interested in collaborating, please reach out via email.
